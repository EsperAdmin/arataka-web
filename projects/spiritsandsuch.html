SpiritsAndSuch.lol is a port of the original re-creation of the website from the show Mob Psycho 100 by Cabrails Ford.


I Saw that the original Wordpress website had been down for some time so I decided to take everything off from the archives on the way back machine and re-make it into an active website again! It's very simple and not complicated AT ALL. I even thought maybe not to add this to this website but I figured why not.


So, yeah, sorry but their is not much to add here. You can visit the website and take a look at it here. Just keep in mind that the guest book page does NOT work and you will get a little popup telling you so if you try to enter any information.
